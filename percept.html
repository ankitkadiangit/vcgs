5 things to know about percept NIPT

  • Percept NIPT is a screening test that can tell you the chance your pregnancy may have a chromosome condition.
  • Percept NIPT gives high chance or low chance results. Most women/couples receive a low chance result.
  • Some women/couples receive a high chance result. In this case, you will be offered further testing to confirm the result.
  • As with all screening tests, there can be incorrect results. This is why all high chance results should be confirmed with further testing.
  • Percept NIPT does not look for all possible genetic or chromosome conditions.
